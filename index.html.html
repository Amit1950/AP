  • Ambedkar Government Polytechnic, Musafirkhana, Amethi is one of the nascent Institutes under aegis of Department of Technical Education, Government of Utter Pradesh. The Institute was established in order to provide diploma level Technical Education to the students of District Amethi and near by areas.
  • Musafirkhana is well connected by Rail and Bus .This is an initiative of the State Government to enhance technical knowledge of people and help industries to get skilled hands from nearby areas.
    • The Institute has started its classes in year 2015 from campus of Sanjay Gandhi Government Polytechnic, Jagdishpur, Amethi. About Our Principal's Message

Welcome to Ambedkar Government Polytechnic, Musafirkhana, Amethi which is situated in a lush green and pollution free campus on Budaun Road. Institute is approachable by Rail & Bus from all directions. Our aim is to impart best possible technical knowledge to the students of the state of Uttar Pradesh to take their productive abilities to the highest level. This is an AICTE approved institute. Our curriculum is developed by IRDT Kanpur, NITTTR Chandigarh & approved by BTE UP Lucknow to the latest contents in respective fields of Engineering & Technology. We thrive for all along the development of students personality and make them a responsible citizen of the country. We go hand in hand with Government initiatives for overall community development. We propagate and work towards “Swaksh Bharat Mission “by adopting the policy of “Green Polytechnic, Clean Polytechnic“. Our emphasis is also on “Saksham Balika Mission” & “Kanya Sumangla Scheme” , “Pragati” & “Saksham (Differently able boys)” scheme in order to bring forward girl students to be part of India’s Industrial revolution. We have a high concern for our Environment & Natural resources therefore we are constantly emphasizing our self & our students to have greatest level of concern for “WATER” saving , to refrain from using single use “PLASTIC”. We have planted 3700 saplings on 9th August 2019 & taking great care of these day in & day out. Thanking you.
